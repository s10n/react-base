# Slugify

## Rules
* [RFC 3986](https://tools.ietf.org/html/rfc3986#section-2.2)
  - Reserved character: `:` `/` `?` `#` `[` `]` `@` `!` `$` `&` `'` `(` `)` `*` `+` `,` `;` `=`
* Unicode (More than alphanumeric)
  - Support CJK
  - Support emoji
